Address

Addesso Imaizumi 4F
Imaizumi 1-chome 21-5
Chuo-ku, Fukuoka city,
Fukuoka Prefecture

〒810-0021 福岡県福岡市中央区今泉１丁目２１−５ アデッソ今泉 4Ｆ

Train

A 5 minute walk west from Nishitetsu Fukuoka Tenjin Station. Exit from Mitsukoshi South Exit and turn right. Cross the street at the second light (Kego Shrine) and continue to the first corner by Chintai Jutaku Service. From there go down the street to the second building on your left. The bar is located on the fourth floor of the Adesso Imaizumi Building.
